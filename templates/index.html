Complete your music collection.

You probably have a lot of music in your iTunes collection, but do you have everything from your favorite artists? This tool will examine your collection and let you know what you can add to make your collection complete.

Please select your system and click the button below to upload your iTunes music library file to our server for processing.

Upload new File

Suggested paths to your file

  • OS X: /Users/{user}/Music/iTunes/iTunes Music Library.xml
  • Windows XP: home + '\My Documents\My Music\iTunes\iTunes Library.xml'
  • Windows Vista, 7, & 8: home + '\Music\iTunes\iTunes Library.xml'

Notes:

  • We're not storing your file or doing anything else with it.
